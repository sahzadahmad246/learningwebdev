    • Easy chicken biryani

    Ingredients

    2 cups basmati rice, 500g chicken, 1 cup yogurt, 2 onions, 2 tomatoes, 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp turmeric, 2 tsp red chili powder, 2 tsp biryani masala, 4 cups water, 3 tbsp oil/ghee, fresh coriander & mint, salt

    Process to make

    1. rinse & soak rice 30 min.
    2. fry onions until golden, set aside.
    3. cook chicken with yogurt, spices & tomatoes until tender.
    4. boil rice 70% done.
    5. layer rice over chicken, top with fried onions, mint & coriander.
    6. cover & cook on low heat 15–20 min (dum).
    7. mix gently and serve hot.

    Kaju Katli

    Ingredients

    2 cups cashews, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1 tbsp ghee, 1/2 tsp cardamom powder, silver vark (optional)

    Process to make

    1. grind cashews into a fine powder (don’t over-blend).
    2. make sugar syrup with sugar & water, 1-string consistency.
    3. add cashew powder, stir continuously on low flame.
    4. cook until soft dough forms, add ghee & cardamom powder.
    5. transfer onto greased plate, knead slightly while warm.
    6. roll flat with a rolling pin, apply silver vark if using.
    7. cut into diamond shapes, cool completely & serve.
